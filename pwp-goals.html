User Persona/User Story

1. Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to create a professional portfolio that I can provide prospective employers with to get a feel for my work.

2. Audience

The audience for this website will be for prospective employers/clients and potentially other developers.

3. Goals

My goal for this website is to produce a clean, well designed portfolio/landing page to act as a gallery to showcase my work, and exhibit a strong design sense. The primary end result from this project is to lead me towards full time work. A secondary goal is to produce something both I and my instructors are proud of and for it to be used as an example in future cohorts.
